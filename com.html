About ComSoc

Empowering Future Communication Engineers

The IEEE Communication Society at BMS College of Engineering is a dynamic student chapter dedicated to advancing the field of communications technology. We bring together passionate students interested in wireless communications, networking, IoT, and emerging technologies.

Through workshops, technical talks, hackathons, and industry interactions, we provide our members with opportunities to enhance their technical skills, network with professionals, and stay updated with the latest developments in communication technology aswell as non technical events.
